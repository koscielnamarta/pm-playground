# KPIs — Thru-Hiking App (PoC)

## Primary goal
Validate whether hikers find value in aggregated long-distance progress tracking.

## Success metrics
- Number of users who upload at least one GPX file: 10
- Number of completed trail segments: 3
- Percentage of users returning to upload another stage: 75

## Engagement signals
- Average number of GPX uploads per user: X
- Time between uploads: 2-4 weeks
- Number of viewed progress maps: X

## Validation criteria (PoC)
- At least X users complete one full upload flow
- At least X users return within 14 days
- Qualitative feedback confirms clarity of progress visualization

## What we intentionally don’t measure (yet)
- Social engagement
- Competition or gamification metrics
- Revenue-related metrics
