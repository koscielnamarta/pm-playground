# Product Brief

_Last updated: 2026-01-13_

# Core User & Use Cases

## Primary user
A person who:
- already uses physical tarot cards
- values intuition, ritual, and reflection
- wants help interpreting spreads more deeply, not just card meanings

## Secondary users (optional)
- Beginners who know card meanings but struggle with full spread interpretation
- Solo practitioners who want a “thinking partner”, not a teacher

## Core use cases

### Use case 1 — Daily or situational reflection
User has a question or emotional state and wants insight.
- Chooses a simple spread
- Lays out physical cards
- Takes a photo
- Receives a holistic interpretation

### Use case 2 — Complex spread interpretation
User performs a multi-card spread and feels unsure about the overall message.
- Wants synthesis across positions and relationships
- Uses AI to see patterns they might miss

### Use case 3 — Learning through practice
User wants to improve their own interpretation skills.
- Compares their intuition with AI output
- Reflects, not blindly follows

### Use case 4 — Emotional grounding
User seeks reassurance or perspective during uncertainty.
- Uses tarot as a reflective tool, not prediction

### Use case 5 - Adding variety to usual tarot practice
User seeks variety in their usual practice
- Uses the app to browse through different spreads (MVP 6 most popular)
